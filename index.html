Can't decide? Spin!

Ever have trouble choosing among too many options? Ever wish you could just have the decision made for you? Ever long for the days of childhood games where a flick of a spinner would tell you exactly where to move your character?

You need Spinner, the brand new iPhone Webapp and Mac OS X Dashboard Widget.

Simply install it on your phone or desktop computer, enter the choices from which you wish to select, click to spin, and say good by to indecision!

Spinner is excellent at choosing among:

  • Places to go
  • Things to eat
  • Beverages to drink
  • Projects to work on
  • ...and more!
